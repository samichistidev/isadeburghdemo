Project

Magic Mind approached me ahead of their 2020 launch to help bring their brand to life digitally. What began as digital brand support evolved into a long-term creative partnership spanning print design, art direction, and marketing creative direction. Over time, I helped shape the visual and strategic foundation behind their direct-to-consumer growth—now serving over 35,000 daily customers—and their retail expansion into 800+ stores nationwide. The collaboration has been rooted in elevating Magic Mind’s mission through consistent, cohesive, and emotionally resonant creative.

I’m a creative director, brand builder, and storyteller driven by the intersection of strategy and emotion. My work lives where clear thinking meets visual and verbal impact. I’m energized diving into a brand from the ground up or evolving one that’s already beloved. As the founder and former creative director of The Social Tale, I’ve led campaigns for brands like De Soi, SideDish, and Colgate, translating ideas into digital and tangible experiences that connect the right consumers with their most beloved brands.

Today, I lead creative at Magic Mind, the world’s first mental performance shot, where I’ve helped shape everything from product launches to visual identity to how the brand speaks to the world in both retail and digital commerce. My background blends design, copywriting, and brand strategy, but what drives me most is finding the throughline: the “why” that makes a brand magnetic.
Outside of work, I’m endlessly curious. I’m drawn to anything that fuels creativity, from photography and interiors to travel and behavioral psychology. I believe great creative starts with observation: paying attention to people, culture, and the small details that surround us on an every day and make us feel human.
